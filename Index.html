Services

Our Services

EVENTS COVERAGE

Through this service, event organizers can hire the magazine crew to cover the event. Covering an event involves running interviews with the attendees as well as photography them producing a leaflet for that particular event. SwaLebs Magazine has had the opportunity to offer this service to the MTN Spotlight Season 1 in 2019

FREELANCE WRITING

SwaLebs Magazine gives local young writers to write motivational articles. These articles are published in the seasonal magazine editions.

ARTISTS MANAGEMENT

The management team signs young and upcoming artists. These artists are provided with a studio in which they can work and release their craft. SwaLebs Magazine artist have since established a platform in which they nurture their skills called The Showcase.

ADVERTISEMENT

The magazine is hired to market businesses and events on its social media platforms and magazine edition releases. Personalized catalogues are also provided.
